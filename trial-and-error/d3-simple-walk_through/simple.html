Scales in d3

Scale is re-range the value from current range to another range with the same relevant spacing

Scale working with "domain" and "range"

Example: d3.scaleLinear().domain([10, 500]).range([100, 2000]). This example show that with limit from 10 => 500. D3, map with the limit 100 => 2000 to show in pixels.

D3 have various form of scale, it depend of what data you will want to show
